Meet the Developer

Why Dumb Exists

Dumb exists to make paywalled apps simpler and more accessible. It strips away the noise and surfaces the core content so you can get what you came for without the extra friction.

The idea is straightforward: take apps that lock information behind paywalls and "dumb them down" into a clean, minimal experience. No clutter, no upsells, no detours.

We build with restraint and a bias for clarity. No dark patterns, no engagement traps, just a clean path from question to answer.

Every release is shaped by real usage: what's blocked, what's confusing, and what can be simplified further.

Dumb is not about reporting or journalism. It's about making paywalled apps easier to use by focusing on the essentials.
